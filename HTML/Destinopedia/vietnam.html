The Land of the Ascending Dragon

Vietnam is a vibrant land of contrasts — from emerald rice paddies and limestone cliffs to bustling cities and peaceful temples. Whether you're cruising through Ha Long Bay, exploring the lantern-lit streets of Hoi An, or tasting authentic pho on a street corner, Vietnam offers unforgettable beauty, culture, and flavor in every corner.

Currency: Vietnamese Dong (VND)
Attraction: Ha Long Bay, Hoi An and Sapa
seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter, rainy

"Lose yourself in a land where mountains meet the sea, lanterns light the night, and every bite tells a
story — discover the soul of Southeast Asia in Vietnam"
