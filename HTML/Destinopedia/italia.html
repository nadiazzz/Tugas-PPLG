The Bel Paese

Italy is a timeless blend of history, art, and irresistible flavors. From the romantic canals of Venice and the ancient ruins of Rome to the rolling hills of Tuscany and the Amalfi Coast’s dramatic views, every corner of Italy tells a story. It’s a destination that captures hearts with its beauty, cuisine, and culture.

Currency: Euro (EUR)
Attraction: Historical landmarks, Colosseum, and Renaissance art
seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter
