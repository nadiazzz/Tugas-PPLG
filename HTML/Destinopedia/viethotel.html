Hagia Sophia

Hagia Sophia, located in Istanbul, is a magnificent architectural masterpiece with a rich history as a cathedral, mosque, and museum. Built in the 6th century, it features a grand dome and intricate mosaics that reflect both Christian and Islamic influences. Today, it stands as a symbol of cultural harmony and a must-visit landmark in Turkey.
