Badrutt's Palace Hotel

Badrutt’s Palace Hotel is a legendary luxury retreat nestled in the heart of St. Moritz, Switzerland. Overlooking the stunning Lake St. Moritz and surrounded by the majestic Alps, the hotel combines timeless elegance with world-class service. With opulent rooms, gourmet dining, and exceptional spa experiences, it's a destination of pure indulgence and Alpine charm.

Address: Via Serlas 27, 7500 St. Moritz, Kanton Graubünden, Switzerland.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 600 CHF

Hotel Bellevue Palace

Hotel Bellevue Palace is a five-star luxury hotel located in the heart of Bern, Switzerland, next to the Swiss Parliament. With its classic elegance and panoramic views of the Alps and Aare River, it offers a refined and historic atmosphere. The hotel has hosted royalty, world leaders, and celebrities. Guests can enjoy fine dining at the acclaimed VUE restaurant and experience Swiss hospitality at its finest.

Address: Kochergasse 3-5, 3011 Bern, Switzerland.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 450 CHF
