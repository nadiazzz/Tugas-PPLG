The Land of the Pharaos

Egypt is a captivating land where ancient history meets desert beauty and Nile River charm. Explore the majestic pyramids of Giza, wander through centuries-old temples, and sail down the Nile like the ancients. With golden sands, rich culture, and timeless monuments, Egypt is a journey through living history.

Currency: Egyptian Pound (EGP)
Attraction: Royal Tombs and one of the seven wonders of the world
seasons: Mild winter and Hot summer

"Step into the land of timeless wonders, where the whispers of pharaohs echo through golden sands — Egypt is
not just a destination, it’s a journey through the dawn of civilization."
