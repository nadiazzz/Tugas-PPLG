Four Seasons Hotel Istanbul at Sultanahmet

Housed in a beautifully restored neoclassical building that once served as a prison, the hotel offers 65 guest rooms and suites arranged around a serene landscaped courtyard. Its prime location places guests within walking distance of iconic landmarks such as the Hagia Sophia, the Blue Mosque, and the Topkapi Palace.

Address: Tevkifhane Sokak No:1, Sultanahmet, 34110 Fatih, Istanbul, Turkey.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 400 TRY

Address: 1, Intizam Sokak, 34367, Beşiktaş, İstanbul, Turkey.
