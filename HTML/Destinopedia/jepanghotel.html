Park Hyatt Tokyo

The Park Hyatt Tokyo is a distinguished five-star hotel situated in the heart of Shinjuku, Tokyo's vibrant commercial and entertainment district. Occupying the upper floors (39th to 52nd) of the Shinjuku Park Tower, the hotel offers panoramic views of the city and, on clear days, Mount Fuji.

Address: 11 Naitomachi, Shinjuku City, Tokyo 160-0014, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 40.000 JPY

The Ritz-Carlton Kyoto

The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto is a luxury hotel situated along the Kamogawa River, offering scenic views of the Higashiyama mountains. The hotel features 134 guest rooms and suites that blend traditional Japanese aesthetics with modern comforts. Accommodations include 600-thread-count linens, handmade soaps, and complimentary internet access.

Address: Kamogawa Nijo-Ohashi Hotori, Kamigyo Ward, Kyoto 602-8580, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 50.000 JPY

Shinjuku Granbell Hotel

Shinjuku Granbell Hotel is a designer hotel located in the heart of Tokyo's vibrant Kabukicho district in Shinjuku. Opened in December 2013, the hotel offers modern and stylish accommodations with unique design concepts crafted by up-and-coming creators from Japan and abroad.

Address: 2-14-5 Kabukicho, Shinjuku City, Tokyo 160-0021, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 10.000 JPY

Hotel New Otani Tokyo

Hotel New Otani Tokyo is a prestigious luxury hotel located in the Kioicho district of Chiyoda City, Tokyo. Established in 1964 to accommodate visitors for the Tokyo Olympics, the hotel has since become an iconic landmark in the city.

Address: 4-1 Kioi-cho, Chiyoda City, Tokyo 102-8578, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 15.000 JPY

Mandarin Oriental Tokyo

Mandarin Oriental, Tokyo is a luxury hotel located in the Nihonbashi Mitsui Tower in Tokyo’s Nihonbashi neighborhood, close to Tokyo Station and Tokyo Stock Exchange. The hotel, opened on 2 December 2005, is managed by Mandarin Oriental Hotel Group.

Address: 2-1-1 Nihonbashi Muromachi, Chuo City, Tokyo 103-8328, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 50.000 JPY

Hotel Nikko Osaka

Hotel Nikko Osaka is a deluxe hotel situated in the heart of Osaka, Japan, facing the renowned tree-lined Mido-suji Avenue. Its prime location offers guests convenient access to the city's main shopping and leisure districts.

Address: 1-3-3 Nishi-Shinsaibashi, Chūō Ward, Osaka 542-0086, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 8.000 JPY

Imperial Hotel Tokyo

Imperial Hotel, Tokyo is a historic luxury hotel located in the Uchisaiwaicho district of Chiyoda, Tokyo. Established in 1890 at the behest of the Japanese aristocracy to accommodate Western visitors, the hotel has undergone several reconstructions, with the current structure offering modern amenities while honoring its rich heritage.

Address: 1-1 Uchisaiwaicho 1-chome, Chiyoda City, Tokyo 100-8558, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 20.000 JPY

The Westin Osaka

The Westin Osaka is a luxury hotel located in the Umeda district of Osaka, Japan. Situated adjacent to the iconic Umeda Sky Building, the hotel offers convenient access to Osaka's major attractions and business centers.

Address: 1-1-20 Oyodonaka, Kita Ward, Osaka 531-0076, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 15.000 JPY

Hotel Gracery Shinjuku

Hotel Gracery Shinjuku is a modern hotel located in the heart of Tokyo's vibrant Shinjuku district. Standing at 130 meters with 30 stories, it serves as a prominent landmark near the former Shinjuku KOMA Theater.

Address: 1-19-1 Kabukicho, Shinjuku City, Tokyo 160-0021, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 10.000 JPY

Karuizawa Prince Hotel

Karuizawa Prince Hotel is a renowned resort complex located in the scenic town of Karuizawa, Nagano Prefecture, Japan. The resort comprises multiple accommodations, including Karuizawa Prince Hotel East, Karuizawa Prince Hotel West, and The Prince Karuizawa, each offering distinct experiences tailored to various guest preferences.

Address: Karuizawa, Karuizawa-machi, Kitasaku-gun, Nagano 389-0102, Japan.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 15.000 JPY
