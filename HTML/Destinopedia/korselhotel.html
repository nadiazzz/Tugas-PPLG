The Shilla Seoul

The Shilla Seoul is a prestigious luxury hotel situated in the heart of Seoul, South Korea. Established in 1979 by the Samsung Group, it seamlessly blends traditional Korean aesthetics with contemporary elegance, offering guests a refined and culturally rich experience.

Address: 249 Dongho-ro, Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 250.000 KRW

Park Hyatt Busan

Park Hyatt Busan is a luxurious five-star hotel located in the heart of Busan's upscale Marine City district. The hotel's architecture is inspired by the graceful curves of a ship's sail, featuring a striking glass façade that offers panoramic views of the ocean and the iconic Gwangan Bridge.

Address: 51, Marine City 1-ro, Haeundae-gu, Busan , South Korea.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 200.000 KRW
