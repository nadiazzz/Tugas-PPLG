The Hexagon

France is a country that enchants with elegance, history, and irresistible charm. From the iconic Eiffel Tower and charming Parisian cafés to the lavender fields of Provence and vineyards of Bordeaux, France offers beauty at every turn. Its world-renowned cuisine, fashion, and art make it a timeless favorite for travelers.

Currency: Euro (EUR)
Attraction: Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and scenic villages
seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter
