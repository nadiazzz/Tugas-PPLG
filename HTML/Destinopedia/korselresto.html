Jinju jip

Jinjujip (진주집) is a renowned Korean restaurant in Seoul, celebrated for its traditional dishes and longstanding culinary heritage. With over 70 years of history, it has become a staple for both locals and visitors seeking authentic Korean flavors.

Address: 22-2 Namdaemunsijang-gil, Jung-gu, , Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 10:00-22:00
Price: Start from 10.000 - 15.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Kkori Gomtang (Oxtail Soup), Kongguksu (Cold Soybean Noodles), Kkori Jjim (Braised Oxtail)

Tosokchon Samgyetang

Tosokchon Samgyetang (토속촌 삼계탕) is one of Seoul’s most iconic restaurants, renowned for its traditional Korean ginseng chicken soup (samgyetang). Located near Gyeongbokgung Palace, it is a must-visit for both locals and tourists seeking an authentic taste of Korean cuisine.​

Address: 5 Jahamun-ro 5-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 10:00-22:00
Price: Start from 15.000 - 20.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Regular Samgyetang, Black Chicken Samgyetang, Black Chicken with Wild Mountain Ginseng

Myeongdong Kyoja

Myeongdong Kyoja (명동교자) is a renowned family-owned restaurant in Seoul, operating since 1966. Located in the bustling Myeongdong district, it has earned a Michelin Bib Gourmand distinction for its high-quality, reasonably priced dishes.

Address: 29-1 Myeongdong 10-gil, Jung-gu , Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 10:30-22:00
Price: Start from 8.000 - 10.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Kalguksu (칼국수), Mandu (만두), Bibim Guksu (비빔국수), Kongguksu (콩국수)

Korea House

Korea House (한국의집) is a premier cultural venue in Seoul that offers visitors an immersive experience of traditional Korean culture, cuisine, and performing arts

Address: Jung-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 09:00-22:00
Price: Start from 30.000 - 80.000 KRW

Gwangjang Market

Gwangjang Market (광장시장) is one of Seoul’s oldest and most vibrant traditional markets, offering a rich tapestry of Korean street food, textiles, and cultural experiences.​

Address: 88 Changgyeonggung-ro, Jongno-gu , Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 09:00-18:00
Price: Start from 3.000 - 10.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Bindaetteok (빈대떡), Mayak Gimbap (마약김밥), Tteokbokki (떡볶이), Kalguksu (칼국수), Mandu (만두), Yukhoe (육회), Fresh Seafood

Noryangjin Fish Market

Noryangjin Fisheries Wholesale Market (노량진수산시장) is Seoul’s largest and oldest seafood market, established in 1927 and relocated to its current location in 1971. It houses over 700 vendors and handles approximately 250 to 300 tons of seafood daily, making it a central hub for both wholesale and retail seafood transactions

Address: 674, Nodeul-ro, Dongjak-gu, Seoul, South Korea.
Operational Hours: 24 Hours
Price: Start from 15.000 - 50.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Fresh seafood dishes

Bulgogi Brothers

Bulgogi Brothers is a casual Korean restaurant known for its authentic bulgogi dishes. First established in Gangnam, Seoul, in 2006, the restaurant has grown to over 40 branches in South Korea, as well as international expansion to Canada, the Philippines, and Malaysia.

Address:46, Tehran-ro 87-gil, Gangnam-gu, B2F Oakwood Hotel, Seoul , South Korea.
Operational Hours: 11:00 - 23:00
Price: Start from 15.000 - 30.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Haemul Gungjung Mandu, Galbijjim, Sogogi Japchae

Palsaik Samgyeopsal

Palsaik Samgyeopsal (팔색삼겹살) is a Korean BBQ restaurant known for its unique concept: serving pork belly (samgyeopsal) in eight different flavors. Each cut of meat is marinated in signature sauces such as ginseng, red wine, pine needles, garlic, curry, miso, herbs, and hot chili peppers, providing a diverse taste experience in one dish.

Address: 21 Myeongdong 3-gil, Jung-gu, Seoul , South Korea.
Operational Hours: 11:00 - 23:00
Price: Start from 20.000 - 40.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Samgyeopsal

Min’s Club

Min’s Club is a fine dining restaurant located in Insadong, Jongno-gu, Seoul, South Korea. The restaurant is housed in a hanok (traditional Korean house) that was once the residence of the nobles of Queen Myeongseong’s descendants in the early 20th century. With a blend of traditional architecture and modern interiors, Min’s Club offers an elegant and historic dining atmosphere.

Address: 23-9, Insadong 10-gil, Jongno-gu, Seoul , South Korea.
Operational Hours: 11:00 - 23:00
Price: Start from 25.000 - 60.000 KRW
Signature Dishes : Tteok-galbi steak, Samgyeopsal jjim, Neobiani steak
