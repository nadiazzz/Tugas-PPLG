The Land of Smiles

Thailand is a tropical paradise where golden temples, bustling street markets, and pristine beaches come together in perfect harmony. From the vibrant energy of Bangkok to the serene islands of the south, and the cultural treasures of Chiang Mai, Thailand offers endless adventure, flavor, and beauty for every kind of traveler.

Currency: Thai Baht (THB)
Attraction: Dynamic city life, temples, and ancient ruins
seasons: Cool, Hot, Rainy
