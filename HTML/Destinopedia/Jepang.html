The Land of the Rising Sun

Japan is a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge modernity. From serene temples and cherry blossoms to neon-lit cities and bullet trains, every part of Japan offers a new adventure. Whether you're drawn to its rich culture, nature, or cuisine, Japan is a destination that leaves a lasting impression.

Currency: Japanese Yen (JPY)
Attraction: Anime culture, temples, fashion and tea ceremonies
seasons: Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter
